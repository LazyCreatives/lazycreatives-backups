  • Talk to music makers like a peer, not a vendor.

Usage

Do

  • Keep clear space around the mark equal to the sloth's head height.
  • Use Sloth Blue for one primary action per screen.
  • Reserve Verified Green strictly for confirmed / passed states.
  • Default to dark surfaces — it's a studio tool.
  • Keep the sloth chill — relaxed poses only.

Don't

  • Recolor the sloth or wordmark outside the approved palette.
  • Stretch, rotate, or drop-shadow the line art.
  • Use gold as a primary CTA — it's attention/warnings now.
  • Use red for anything other than risk / errors.
  • Place the full-colour logo on busy photos.
